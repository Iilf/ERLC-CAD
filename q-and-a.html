Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before hitting the streets.

What is VDM and RDM?

VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) is using a vehicle as a weapon without RP. RDM (Random Deathmatch) is killing someone without a valid roleplay reason. Both are community violations.

How do I apply for a job?

Civilian jobs can be found at the Job Center in-game. For whitelisted jobs like PD or EMS, you must apply on our Discord server.

Is a microphone required?

Yes. We are a "Serious RP" community. High-quality communication is essential to maintain immersion for everyone.

What is Powergaming?

Powergaming is performing actions that are impossible in real life or forcing roleplay on others without giving them a chance to react.
